Reclaiming Africa’s Narrative

A cultural revolution in broadcast training

The Narrative Nexus

Reclaiming Africa’s Voice in a Global Symphony

A Legacy of Untold Stories

At the core of Africa lies a tapestry of untold stories — narratives of innovation, resilience, creativity, and community — too often obscured by external perspectives. The Broadcast Training Academy is here to rewrite this story, amplifying Africa’s authentic voice through a global platform.

Empowering a New Generation

We’re building a continent-wide movement of young African storytellers — trained, empowered, and inspired to share their unfiltered truths. Through state-of-the-art broadcast training, mentorship, and media literacy, we equip the next generation with the tools to create content that resonates with Africa’s true essence, unshackled from foreign narratives.

A Cultural Revolution

ABAis more than an academy; it’s a cultural revolution — a bold mission to reclaim the African narrative and broadcast it to the world with unapologetic authenticity. We’re redefining global media through the lens of Africa’s visionaries.

Inspiring Global Impact

Together, we’re crafting media that transcends borders — not just to inform, but to inspire. This is Africa, told by its own people, on a global stage that shapes the future of storytelling.

“This is Africa, told by its own people — a global narrative reborn.”

Global Expertise

OlorunRokan Ojomo

OlorunRokan Ojomo is a globally recognized video journalist, documentary filmmaker, and videographer/photographer with over 13 years of transformative media experience. He has collaborated with world-class news agencies such as DW News, BBC News, CGTN News, and African News, as well as international organizations including WHO, UNICEF, GIZ, the European Union, and IOM. A graduate of the London School of Photography and the London School of Journalism, OlorunRokan also contributed 8 years to the Global Fund Project in Nigeria, advancing global health initiatives.
